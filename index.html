EKG View

Innovative venture by a group of doctors who share a vision that ecg technology should be easy, powerful and accessible to everyone. Taking advantage of the latest advancements in cloud computing the idea of bringing interactive readings through any browser to people around the world was made a reality. EKG View is also a social community for healthcare workers to interact, learn, share ideas and work together to improve their skills.

How it works

Criticize and argue, comment and add important details, be active! Together, all these interactions make up the precious nuggets of knowledge that can be used by various people all over the world. Our community is built solely on non-profit interactions of talented people who are helping each other prosper. We don't pay authors and don't buy charts. Everything you see is genuine grassroots analysis. We believe that together, we can make ECGs easier and quicker to read. Start sharing your ideas today!
